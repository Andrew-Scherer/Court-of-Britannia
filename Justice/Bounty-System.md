**Victim-Funded Retribution**

## üìú The Philosophy
The Crown does not subsidize justice. If you are murdered, **your gold** pays for the vengeance. 

Bounties are not printed from thin air; they are extracted from the victim's bank at the moment of death and placed on the killer's head.

---

## ‚ö∞Ô∏è Bounty Generation (Voluntary Contribution)

When a **Crown-Aligned Vassal** is murdered, they are presented with a final choice before their ghost is released.

### The Death Choice (60 Seconds)
The victim has **60 seconds** to decide how much of their own wealth they wish to pledge towards vengeance.
*   **Slider:** 0% to 50% of Global Wealth (Bank + Gold on Person).
*   **0% (Forgiveness/Apathy):** No gold is taken. No bounty is created. The murder remains a statistic only.
*   **50% (Maximum Vengeance):** Huge sum extracted from the victim's estate to fund the hunter pot.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **Vassals Only**
> This option is **only** available to Crown-Aligned players. If a Non-Aligned player is killed, the death is "Warfare," not "Murder." No choice is offered. No bounty is possible.

### The Source
The gold is **extracted immediately** from the victim's Bank Box. If the victim cannot afford the pledge (e.g., they pledge 50% but have 0g), the bounty fails to generate. Bounty gold comes from the victim, not the Crown.

---

## üîç Activation (The Forensic Key)

A bounty does **not** exist simply because a murder occurred. It must be **proven**.

1.  **The Murder**: The victim dies. Body decomposes.
2.  **The Window**: A [Forensic Investigator] has 24 hours to find the corpse.
3.  **The Split**:
    *   **If Unsolved**: The body rots. The "Potential Bounty" vanishes. **0g** is placed on the killer.
    *   **If Solved**: The Investigator files a report. The Bounty is **Activated**.

### The "Solve" Mechanic
A crime is mechanically **Solved** when a Forensic Investigator successfully uses the forensic skill to attribute the death to a specific character in the [Mortuary Rolls].

*   **First Solver Wins**: Only the **first** investigator to actively solve the crime (retrieve the signature) is eligible for the 30% cut. Subsequent investigators get nothing.
*   **Visual Confirmation**: Upon a successful solve, the corpse's tooltip updates globally to: *"The corpse of [Victim], murdered by [Killer]"*.

### The 70/30 Locked Split
Once activated, the bounty is irrevocably divided:

*   **70% (The Hunter's Pot)**: 175g (from example) goes to the **Executor**.
*   **30% (The Investigator's Lock)**: 75g (from example) is **Locked** for the **Investigator**.

> **Note**: The Investigator does *not* get paid immediately. They get paid **when the killer is brought to justice**. Their payout is attached to the criminal's fate.

## ‚öîÔ∏è Distribution (The Payout)

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **License to Kill**
> Only **Crown-Aligned Vassals** are eligible to claim Bounties or Forensic Rewards. Non-Aligned players are considered "Unlicensed." They may kill Red players for sport or safety, but the Crown will **not** pay them.

When a **Licensed** Bounty Hunter kills a Red player and turns in the Head:

1.  **The Hunter**: Receives **100% of the Available Hunter Pot** (The 70% portions of all murders committed by that Red).
2.  **The Investigators**: The Treasury automatically releases the **Locked 30% portions** to the bank accounts of every Investigator who contributed a solve to that criminal's record.

## ‚öñÔ∏è Summary
*   **No Taxes**: The Crown pays nothing.
*   **No Inflation**: Gold is transferred, not created.
*   **No Solves, No Bounties**: Uninvestigated crimes are free crimes.
