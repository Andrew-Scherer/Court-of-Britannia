## âš”ï¸ Mutual War Legitimacy

War is only legitimate when **both guilds declare war on each other**.

- **Mutual War**: Combat between the two guilds is treated as wartime conflict (not criminal by default).
- **One-Sided War**: If only one guild declares, **all attacks remain crimes** and produce normal criminal status.

This prevents predatory war declarations and preserves criminality as the boundary between sanctioned conflict and banditry.

A successful Monarch must manage these tensions, not eliminate them:
- **Balance Factions**: Staff the court with rivals to prevent any single group from becoming too powerful to control.
