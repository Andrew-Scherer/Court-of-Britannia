### The Intervention Boundary
**Staff will NOT intervene in:**
- **Political Disputes**: If a King is a tyrant, use the [Justice System] or [Bounty System].
- **Economic Warfare**: If a guild is being squeezed by taxes or liens, use [Diplomacy].
- **In-Game Losses**: Staff will not "undo" accidental deaths, misplaced items, or bad trades.
- **Office Vacancies**: If no player is filling a role, the system persists in a state of neglect. Staff do not "fill in" for players.

In a permadeath environment, **History is Linear**.
- There are no "rollbacks" for character deaths.
- There are no "prizes" returned if they are stolen or lost.
- **Why?** If a GM can undo a loss, the weight of every other action in the shard is halved.
