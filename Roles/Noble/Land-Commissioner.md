## ğŸ˜ï¸ Interaction with Royal Property
- **Role-Linked Offices**: Certain properties (Barracks, Treasury, Jail) are linked to specific roles. The Commissioner ensures these access rights update instantly when officials change.
- **Royal Tenure**: The Commissioner manages discretionary access to palaces and secondary royal chambers on behalf of the Crown using a two-zone model:
