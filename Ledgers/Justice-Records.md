## ğŸ“œ Arrest Records (The History)
The **Arrest Records** are the permanent record of every detention conducted by the Guard.

### Granular Data Tracked:
- **Arrest ID**: Sequential identifier for the event.
- **Reporting Officer**: The specific Guard or Deputy who initiated the arrest.
- **Suspect Account**: The player character taken into custody.
- **Primary Charge**: (Theft, Assault, Trespassing, etc.).
- **Bail Amount**: Gold required for immediate release (if applicable).
- **Incident Timestamp**: Real-world date and time of booking.
- **Forensic Link**: Cross-reference to any [Forensic Signatures] used as evidence.

## â›“ï¸ Jail Ledger (The Present)
A public accounting of current custody, located at the Britain Jail entrance.

### Granular Data Tracked:
- **Prisoner ID**: The unique session ID for the current sentence.
- **Entry Time**: When the prisoner was physically placed in a cell.
- **Anticipated Release**: Calculated timer based on fixed sentence duration.
- **Labor Debt**: Total [Labor Units] (e.g., Ore to be mined) required for release.
- **Labor Progress**: Current units completed by the prisoner.
- **Health Status**: Current vitals to prevent "death in custody" without record.

## âš–ï¸ Forensic Signatures
Recordings of evidence gathered by skilled investigators.

### Granular Data Tracked:
- **Signature ID**: Unique hash of the crime scene data.
- **Investigator Identity**: The player who retrieved the signature.
- **Investigator Skill**: The skill level at the time of retrieval (affects data detail).
- **Victim Identity**: The character whose death/assault was investigated.
- **Suspect Identification**: (If 61+ skill) The name of the perpetrator.
- **Weapon Signature**: The specific weapon type used (e.g., *Double Axe of Vanquishing*).
