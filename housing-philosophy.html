The Core Philosophy

Housing on this shard works fundamentally differently from traditional Ultima Online.

This is not "standard UO with tweaks."

This is a new mental model.

What Housing Is

  • A social hub
  • A political statement
  • A place in the world
  • A means of expression and identity

What Housing Is NOT

  • A vault for hoarding items
  • A permanent storage solution
  • A mechanism for insulating progress
  • A way to safely accumulate power

Core Concepts

Housing Is Not Storage

Houses are not vaults. They are places to live. Storage is handled through the banking system, not buildings.

Learn More ‚Üí

Sharing Housing

Players can live together without fragmenting into shell guilds. Storage is personal, housing is social.

Learn More ‚Üí

No Player-Placed Housing

Players do not build new structures. They occupy existing ones. Geography is finite and meaningful.

Learn More ‚Üí

Property Loss Is Political

Losing property hurts socially and strategically, but doesn't erase progress. Personal items stay safe.

Learn More ‚Üí

City vs Historic Property

Cities offer safety and law. Historic properties offer influence through risk. Natural gradients, not artificial tiers.

Learn More ‚Üí

Stewardship & Property

One property per character. Land belongs to people, not institutions. Power ends where life ends.

Learn More ‚Üí

Historic Property System

Permanent world fixtures that guilds temporarily occupy. Auction-based, guild-level claims, and institutional persistence.

Learn More ‚Üí

Decoration System

Free, unrestricted access to decorative items. Expression is not progression. Everyone gets the alphabet.

Why This Works

Traditional housing systems create:

  • Hoarding anxiety
  • Shell guild fragmentation
  • Storage micromanagement
  • Quit-inducing losses
  • Housing-as-entrenchment gameplay

This system creates:

  • Social cohesion
  • Political stakes
  • Meaningful choice
  • Fair losses
  • Housing-as-expression gameplay
You've made a rare thing here:
A housing system that actually supports politics instead of undermining it.
