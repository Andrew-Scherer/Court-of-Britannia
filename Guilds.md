## Guilds May Form Factions

When multiple guilds choose to unite for political, military, or economic reasons, they may form a **faction**.


> **Note**
> **âš”ï¸ What Are Factions?**
>
> **Factions are NOT a separate organization type.**
> Factions are **emergent alliances of guilds**â€”social, fragile, and kept brutally lightweight by design.
> **What factions provide:**
> - Shared banner/emblem (visual identity)
> - Shared faction name (cosmetic tag)
> **What factions do NOT have:**
> - No faction treasury
> - No faction upkeep
> - No faction leadership structure
> - No faction courts or treaties
> **If a faction collapses, nothing breaks mechanically.** Individual guilds continue to exist.
