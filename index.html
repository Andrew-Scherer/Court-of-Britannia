Season 1: Britain Only

The shard launches with Britain as the entire playable world. This is intentional:

  • Density creates drama ‚Äî everyone shares the same spaces
  • Deaths are witnessed ‚Äî rumors spread fast
  • Political stakes feel immediate
  • Small population feels intense, not empty

Other cities exist in lore but are distant, abstract, and politically reactive rather than active. Britain is where power is decided.
